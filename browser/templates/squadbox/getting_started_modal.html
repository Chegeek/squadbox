Getting Started

1. Add one or more moderators. Your moderators will review messages for you to add tags, summaries, and approve/reject messages. Click here to invite moderators.

2. Direct messages that you want moderated to your Squadbox account. If you use Gmail, connect your account to automatically create filters and generate whitelist suggestions. If you don't use Gmail, you should set up a filter that forwards messages from your inbox to {{group_info.group.name}}@squadbox.org unless if they are sent to the mailing list (or have list-id) {{ filter_hash }}.

3. Add trusted senders to your whitelist. Emails from trusted senders will be automatically approved. You can add addresses manually, or import your contacts to get suggestions if you use Gmail.

4. Add any sender you want to block to your blacklist.

5. Check out our resource guide for information on online security, protecting yourself from online harassment, additional support networks, and more.
